  Description:
    Abstract:
      This map layer shows Amtrak intercity railroad passenger terminals in the 
      United States and Canada.  It includes train stations and thruway bus 
      stations.  There are no Amtrak stations in Alaska or Hawaii.  The data are 
      a subset of data available from the Research and Innovative Technology 
      Administration's Bureau of Transportation Statistics (RITA/BTS) 
      <http://www.bts.gov/gis/>.
    Purpose: 
      These data are intended for geographic display and analysis at the 
      national level, and for large regional areas.  The data should be 
      displayed and analyzed at scales appropriate for 1:2,000,000-scale data.  
      No responsibility is assumed by the National Atlas of the United States in 
      the use of these data.

Data_Quality_Information:
  Attribute_Accuracy:
    Attribute_Accuracy_Report:
      Attribute data were extracted from the station listings in the 
      Spring/Summer 2000 Amtrak National and Summer/Fall 2000 Amtrak Northeast 
      Timetables, and reflect station characteristics as of July 9, 2000.  
      Additional data, as required, were obtained from earlier year timetables.
  Logical_Consistency_Report: 
    No checks for topological consistency were performed on this map layer. 
  Completeness_Report:
    The database includes all Amtrak railroad and thruway bus stations listed in 
    the Amtrak timetables mentioned above.  Regional commuter railroad stations
    that are not also served by Amtrak are not included in this version; those   
    stations are currently included in the Fixed-Guideway Transit Network  
    database.  The database does not include information on passenger boardings; 
    those data are considered to be proprietary by Amtrak.
